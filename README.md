### Insights:
- **Summer Olympics**: Host countries win significantly more gold medals when hosting (p = 0.0003)
- **Winter Olympics**: No statistically significant host advantage (p = 0.1730)
- **Russia** showed the largest increase in gold medals when hosting Summer Games
- **USA** also benefits significantly from home-field advantage

---

## Why the Difference?

Possible explanations for Summer vs Winter disparity:
- Fewer Winter Olympics host countries to analyze
- Winter sports are often timed (objective) vs judged (subjective)
- Winter sports require specific geography, limiting competitive countries
- Indoor Summer venues may amplify home crowd effect
